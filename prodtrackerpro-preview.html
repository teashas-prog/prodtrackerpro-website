Features

Everything you need to
run your production floor

One app replaces your spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and paper logs. Your team enters data on their phones — you see everything in real time.

📋

Daily Production Tracking

Log production counts per product, per day. See what was made, how much, and track against targets. Your team enters it — you see it instantly.

🧪

Raw Materials & Inventory

Track stock levels, log purchases with tax calculations, flag what's running low, and see exactly what you need to order — all categorised your way.

📍

GPS Clock-In

Staff must be physically at the factory to clock in. Geofenced time tracking eliminates buddy-punching and ensures accountability.

🚚

Orders & Deliveries

Manage customer orders, track deliveries by product, log returns with reasons, and connect orders to deliveries with one tap.

📦

Packing Calculator

Define what goes into each packaged unit. The app tells you exactly how many you can pack based on today's production — and logs what was packed.

Role-Based Access

Six roles from CEO to production team. Each person sees only what they need. Recipes stay private. Settings stay locked. You stay in control.
