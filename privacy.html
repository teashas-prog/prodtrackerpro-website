5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud) servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Database security rules: Firebase security rules ensure that each company can only access its own data
  • Access control: Role-based permissions limit what each user can view and modify within the application
  • Authentication: User accounts are secured with email/password authentication
  • No plaintext passwords: Passwords are hashed and never stored in readable form

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. GPS and Location Privacy

We take location privacy seriously. Here is exactly how GPS data works in Production Tracker Pro:

  • GPS is only activated when a user taps "Clock In Now" or "Clock Out Now"
  • We collect a single GPS reading at that moment — latitude, longitude, and distance from the factory
  • We do not track movement, routes, or location history
  • We do not collect location data in the background
  • GPS data is visible only to company administrators (CEO and Manager roles)
  • The GPS geofence radius is configured by the company administrator
  • Staff members can see their own clock-in location but not other staff members' locations

Employer responsibility: If you are a company administrator using GPS clock-in, you are responsible for informing your employees about location tracking and obtaining their consent as required by applicable employment and privacy laws in your jurisdiction.

  • Firebase session: Authentication tokens are stored locally to keep you logged in
  • We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies
