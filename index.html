Features
Everything you need to
run your factory

From raw materials to final delivery — track every step of your manufacturing process in real-time.

📊

Production Tracking

Log daily production counts, set targets, track opening and closing stock. Color-coded progress bars show what's on track and what's behind.

📦

Cases & Packing

Track cases packed with units-per-case calculations. Packing calculator shows what you can pack from today's production. Single units for loose items.

🧪

Raw Materials

4-view system: Stock levels, Needed checklist, Purchases with tax tracking, and Gap analysis. Auto-deduction from recipes when production is logged.

📍

GPS Clock-In

Staff can only clock in within your factory's geofence radius. Prevents buddy punching. Per-staff GPS verification with automatic overtime flagging.

🧾

Recipe Costing

Link raw materials to recipes. See cost per batch and per unit automatically. When you log production, materials deduct from inventory based on recipes.

🚚

Orders & Deliveries

Log customer orders, tap "Deliver" when ready — the form pre-fills. Track delivery expenses, returns, and customer history. Overstock warnings built in.

👥

Role-Based Access

6 role levels from CEO to Regular Team. Each role only sees the tabs they need. Supervisors can't edit recipes. Sales can't change production data.

📱

WhatsApp Reports

Share daily production summaries, staff hours, and delivery logs directly to WhatsApp — where your team already communicates. One-tap sharing.

🌙

Works Offline

No internet? No problem. Data saves locally and syncs when you're back online. Dark mode, undo functionality, and real-time sync across all devices.
