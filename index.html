Features
Everything you need to
run your factory

From raw materials to final delivery — track every step of your manufacturing process in real-time.

📊

Production Tracking

Log daily production counts, set targets, and track opening and closing stock. Color-coded progress bars show what's on track and what's behind. Batch codes keep everything traceable.

📦

Cases & Packing

Track cases packed with automatic units-per-case calculations. The built-in packing calculator shows exactly how many units you can pack from today's production run.

🧪

Raw Materials

Four-view inventory system: Stock levels, Needed checklist, Purchases with tax tracking, and Gap analysis. Materials auto-deduct from stock when production is logged.

📍

GPS Clock-In

Staff can only clock in within your factory's GPS geofence. Eliminates buddy punching. Each person's location is verified individually with automatic overtime flagging.

🧾

Recipe Costing

Link raw materials to product recipes. Instantly see cost per batch and cost per unit. When production is logged, materials automatically deduct based on recipe quantities.

🚚

Orders & Deliveries

Receive customer orders, tap "Deliver" when ready — the delivery form pre-fills automatically. Track delivery expenses including fuel, toll, and shipping costs.

👥

Role-Based Access

Six role levels from CEO to Regular Team. Each person sees only the tabs relevant to their job. Managers can't access recipes. Sales can't modify production data.

📱

WhatsApp Reports

Generate and share daily production summaries, staff attendance, delivery logs, and material usage reports directly to WhatsApp with a single tap.

🌐

Works Offline

Factory internet is unreliable? No problem. Data saves locally and syncs automatically when connectivity returns. Real-time sync across all devices when online.

Before Production Tracker Pro, daily production was tracked on paper, materials were counted manually, and staff attendance relied on the honour system.

✓
GPS clock-in eliminated buddy punching — 8 staff members now verify their location within 100 metres of the factory before clocking in. No more signing in for absent colleagues.
✓
Recipe costing revealed true product costs — linking 46 raw materials to product recipes showed the exact cost per batch and cost per unit, enabling better pricing decisions.
✓
Auto material deduction saved hours of manual counting — when production counts are entered, raw materials automatically deduct based on recipe quantities. No more end-of-day stock discrepancies.
