Features
Everything you need to run your factory

From raw materials to final delivery — track every step of your manufacturing process in real-time.

📊

Production Tracking

Log daily production counts, set targets, and track opening and closing stock. Color-coded progress bars show what's on track.

📦

Cases & Packing

Track cases packed with automatic units-per-case calculations. Packing calculator shows how many units you can pack from today's production.

🧪

Raw Materials

Four-view inventory: Stock levels, Needed checklist, Purchases with tax tracking, and Gap analysis. Auto-deduction when production is logged.

📍

GPS Clock-In

Staff can only clock in within your factory's GPS geofence. Eliminates buddy punching. Each person's location is verified individually.

🧾

Recipe Costing

Link raw materials to recipes. See cost per batch and per unit. Materials automatically deduct based on recipe quantities.

🚚

Orders & Deliveries

Receive customer orders, tap "Deliver" when ready — the form pre-fills automatically. Track fuel, toll, and shipping costs.

👥

Role-Based Access

Six role levels from CEO to Regular Team. Each person sees only the tabs relevant to their job. Full access control built in.

📱

WhatsApp Reports

Generate and share daily production summaries, staff attendance, and delivery logs directly to WhatsApp with a single tap.

🌐

Works Offline

Data saves locally and syncs automatically when connectivity returns. Real-time sync across all devices when online.
